How does it work?

The I Swear To Myself system allows you to create a decentralized "TODO List", powered by 4 main actions: Create, Edit, Write and Seal, which work as follows:


Create an ISTM List

This revolutionary system, allows you to connect your Solana wallet and create a new Account that will be used to store your goals.



Edit the size of the List

As the saying goes, "data on the blockchain is immutable,". It's important to allocate enough space for your list to fit all of your goals.





Write all your ambitious & goals

Here is where you can get creative. Challenge yourself to run that extra mile, increase your chess rating, learn how to play the guitar, etc.



Seal your ISTM List, add a small reward and send it to the network

To help boost the motivation, make sure to store a small reward for yourself, that you can enjoy at the end of 2023.
